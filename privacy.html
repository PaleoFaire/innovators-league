Privacy Policy

Effective date: April 17, 2026 · Draft — pending final legal review

This policy explains what personal information The Innovators League collects, how we use it, and the choices you have.

1. Information we collect

  • Account data you provide: email address, display name, password hash (via Supabase authentication).
  • Usage data: pages viewed, watchlist selections, and basic analytics (referrer, device type, anonymized IP).
  • Support correspondence: if you email us, we keep the message to respond.

We do not ask for, collect, or store payment card numbers, government IDs, or bank account details.

2. How we use information

  • To operate the platform (authentication, saved views, personalized recommendations).
  • To improve the product based on aggregated usage.
  • To communicate important service changes.

We do not sell your personal information.

3. Sharing

We share personal information only with infrastructure providers we need to run the service (Supabase for auth, GitHub for hosting, Cloudflare for CDN). Each is bound by its own privacy commitments.

4. Cookies & local storage

We use local storage and cookies to keep you signed in and remember preferences (watchlists, filter settings). You can clear these at any time in your browser.

5. Data retention

We keep account data while your account is active. If you delete your account, we remove personal data within 30 days, retaining only anonymized aggregate analytics.

6. Your rights

You may access, correct, export, or delete your account data at any time by emailing contact@rationaloptimistsociety.com. Residents of the EEA, UK, and California have additional rights under GDPR/UK GDPR/CCPA which we honor.

7. Security

We use industry-standard safeguards (TLS in transit, authenticated database access, principle of least privilege). No service is perfectly secure — if you believe your account is compromised, contact us immediately.

8. Children

The service is not directed to children under 18 and we do not knowingly collect data from them.

9. Changes

We may update this policy. Material changes will be announced on the site.

10. Contact

Questions? Email contact@rationaloptimistsociety.com.
