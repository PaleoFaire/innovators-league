# US Trip — Top 100 Hard-Tech Companies to Meet (2026-05-22)

The 100 highest-priority **US-based, still-independent, physical-hard-tech** companies from the newly-added database, ranked for in-person meetings on your upcoming US trip.

## How this was built

Started from **568 newly-added companies**. To get a real "who to meet" list I had to verify location + current status on the ~200 thin VC-sweep entries that had no location data — so **14 parallel research agents** confirmed HQ city, founder, stage, raise, and operating status for 199 companies.

The funnel:
- **568** newly-added → filter to hard physical tech → **386**
- Drop the **45** that turned out **acquired / public / defunct** (the VC sweeps had pulled in years of exits — Luxtera→Cisco, Transphorm→Renesas, Desktop Metal + Markforged→Nano Dimension, Graphcore→SoftBank, Covariant→Amazon, Embark→Applied Intuition, Dedrone→Axon, Fox Robotics + Veo→Symbotic; Bowery / Tempo / Shapeways / AESCAPE defunct; Tesla / Sunrun / Archer / ESS public)
- Drop the **54 non-US** (can't meet on a US trip — UFORCE/UK, Halter/NZ, PhysicsX/UK, Diraq/AU, EdgeCortix/JP, etc.)
- Drop **11** food/pharma/consumer (Impossible Foods, Whoop, Rakuten Medical, Aidoc, Savor…)
- **149 usable** → **top 100** ranked below.

All 45 dead entries were also **culled from the live database** (commit 22685fc5).

**Scoring:** stage fit (seed/A best for our $100-250K check) · tier-1 VC · hard-sector weight · trip-hub accessibility · momentum (defense contracts, FDA clearance, American Dynamism).
