What it is

A free, public-access intelligence platform aggregating signal from the noise around defense, space, nuclear, semiconductor, robotics, and advanced-manufacturing companies. Data is sourced from primary feeds — SEC EDGAR, SAM.gov, USPTO, FAA, NRC, openFDA, ClinicalTrials.gov, NSF, NIH, DOE, arXiv, RSS, and direct company press rooms — and rebuilt automatically every hour.

The Innovators League is the deal-flow layer that powers ROS investment decisions, public-market basket curation, and the weekly Frontier Daily intelligence briefing.

“The companies rebuilding the American industrial base deserve a deal engine that is faster than press releases and more honest than promotional decks.”
— Stephen McBride, Founder

Founder bio

Stephen McBride

Stephen McBride is the founder of RiskHedge and the Rational Optimist Society (ROS). He has spent more than a decade writing on frontier technology and capital markets for an audience of 50,000+ investors and founders. The Innovators League is his bet that the next generation of consequential American companies need an intelligence platform built by someone who actually visits the factories, calls the founders, and writes what they believe.

Based in Abu Dhabi; reachable on Twitter as @stephenmcb.

About Rational Optimist Society (ROS)

ROS is a media-and-research firm covering the technologies reshaping the physical world: nuclear, defense, space, semiconductors, advanced manufacturing, autonomous systems, and biotech. The weekly newsletter publishes founder profiles, factory visits, and deal signals to a global audience of investors and builders. ROS is also building an investment fund focused on the same companies it covers.
