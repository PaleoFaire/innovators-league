PROPRIETARY INTELLIGENCE

Innovator Score™

Our proprietary composite metric ranking frontier tech companies across 6 dimensions. The definitive measure of startup potential.

üìä How We Calculate Innovator Score

Each company is scored 1-10 across six dimensions, then weighted to produce a composite score (0-100). Scores are updated quarterly based on:

  • Technology Moat (25%): Patent portfolio, trade secrets, technical differentiation, and defensibility against competitors
  • Momentum (25%): Recent funding, partnerships, contracts, product launches, and growth trajectory
  • Team Pedigree (15%): Founder backgrounds, key hires, "mafia" connections (SpaceX, Palantir, etc.)
  • Market Gravity (15%): TAM size, market timing, regulatory tailwinds, and category leadership potential
  • Capital Efficiency (10%): Valuation relative to capital raised ‚Äî value created per dollar invested
  • Gov't Traction (10%): Federal contracts, SBIR awards, agency relationships, and defense budget exposure
