🎯 Frontier Index™ Methodology (v3.0)

Purpose-built for defense, space, energy, and deep tech. Unlike generic startup scores, we weight what actually matters in frontier markets:

25% Capital Momentum

Funding velocity, round size vs. sector median, investor quality tier, time since last raise

25% Government Traction

DoD/DoE contracts, SBIR/STTR awards, security clearances, prime contractor relationships

20% Technical Moat

TRL advancement rate, patent portfolio, ITAR/export control advantages, deep tech barriers

15% Operator DNA

Founder clearance level, defense/gov experience, technical depth, mafia network strength

15% Strategic Alignment

NDAA relevance, allied nation partnerships, critical supply chain position, regulatory tailwinds

👑 90+ Elite 🔥 80-89 Exceptional ⚡ 70-79 Strong 📈 60-69 Emerging

Scores updated weekly. Each assessment includes documented evidence, prior period comparison, and trend analysis.

SECTOR INTELLIGENCE

Sector Momentum Index

Real-time momentum scores across all frontier tech sectors.

Methodology: Each sector's momentum score (0-100) is calculated from 5 weighted factors: Funding Velocity (30%) — rate of new capital entering the sector over trailing 90 days; Deal Count (20%) — number of disclosed funding rounds; Median Round Size Growth (15%) — change in typical deal size vs. prior quarter; New Company Formation (15%) — startups founded or emerging from stealth; Signal Strength (20%) — aggregate of hiring velocity, news sentiment, and patent filings across sector companies. Scores update weekly.

TRANSPARENCY

Our Data Methodology

How we source, verify, and maintain the intelligence in this database.

📊 Data Sources

  • Company Data: Primary research, press releases, SEC filings (EDGAR), Crunchbase, PitchBook cross-reference
  • Funding Rounds: Press releases, regulatory filings, investor announcements, verified within 48 hours
  • Government Contracts: SAM.gov, USASpending.gov, FPDS, SBIR.gov - updated daily
  • Patents: USPTO PatentsView API, Google Patents - updated weekly
  • News & Signals: 50+ curated RSS feeds, SEC EDGAR, major tech/defense publications

✅ Verification Process

  • Multi-source confirmation: Key data points require 2+ independent sources
  • Direct outreach: Companies can verify/correct their own data via Partner Portal
  • Freshness tracking: Each data category tracks its last update and source
  • Editorial review: Human review of all company additions and major updates

🔄 Update Frequency

  • Real-time: SEC filings, major news alerts
  • Daily: Government contracts, funding announcements
  • Weekly: Patent filings, headcount estimates, Frontier Index scores
  • Monthly: Full database audit and quality review

⚠️ Limitations & Disclaimers

  • Private company valuations are estimates from secondary market data and press reports
  • Headcount data may lag actual numbers by 1-3 months
  • Some metrics (revenue, margins) are not publicly disclosed for private companies
  • This is not investment advice. Always conduct your own due diligence.
