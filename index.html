Our Data Methodology

How we source, verify, and maintain the intelligence in this database.

üìä Data Sources

  • Company Data: Primary research, press releases, SEC filings (EDGAR), Crunchbase, PitchBook cross-reference
  • Funding Rounds: Press releases, regulatory filings, investor announcements, verified within 48 hours
  • Government Contracts: SAM.gov, USASpending.gov, FPDS, SBIR.gov - updated daily
  • Patents: USPTO PatentsView API, Google Patents - updated weekly
  • News & Signals: 50+ curated RSS feeds, SEC EDGAR, major tech/defense publications

‚úÖ Verification Process

  • Multi-source confirmation: Key data points require 2+ independent sources
  • Direct outreach: Companies can verify/correct their own data via Partner Portal
  • Freshness tracking: Each data category tracks its last update and source
  • Editorial review: Human review of all company additions and major updates

üîÑ Update Frequency

  • Real-time: SEC filings, major news alerts
  • Daily: Government contracts, funding announcements
  • Weekly: Patent filings, headcount estimates, Frontier Index scores
  • Monthly: Full database audit and quality review

‚ö†Ô∏è Limitations & Disclaimers

  • Private company valuations are estimates from secondary market data and press reports
  • Headcount data may lag actual numbers by 1-3 months
  • Some metrics (revenue, margins) are not publicly disclosed for private companies
  • This is not investment advice. Always conduct your own due diligence.
