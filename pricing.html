FAQ

Where does your data come from?

Primary sources only: SEC EDGAR, SAM.gov, USPTO, FAA, NRC, openFDA, ClinicalTrials.gov, NASA, NSF, NIH, DOE, arXiv, and company IR/press rooms. Every signal links back to the original record.

Do you offer discounts for startups and students?

Yes — email us with your company or university email address and we’ll get you set up on Analyst at 50% off.

How often is data refreshed?

News signals hourly. Jobs, stocks, and high-velocity feeds every six hours. Regulatory, patent, and grant data daily. Some extended feeds (patent velocity, executive moves) weekly.

Can I cancel any time?

Yes. Monthly plans can be cancelled from your account settings and run until the end of the current billing period.

Is there an API?

Team and Enterprise plans include API access. Public data endpoints and documentation are in private beta — email us to join.
